Our Story

Mrs. Krishna Bansal’s vision for education

Mrs. Krishna Bansal, a doting mother to two daughters, saw ambitious young girls struggle to pursue higher education in Haldwani. They couldn’t find proper housing and as a result, their education would suffer. In 2010, Mrs. Bansal took it upon herself to give them

  • A safe place to stay
  • A chance to pursue their dreams
  • A place they could call home
